Tesla Model S

The Tesla Model S is an all-electric luxury sedan that combines cutting-edge technology, jaw-dropping acceleration, and impressive range. With advanced Autopilot capabilities and an ultra-modern design, the Model S is redefining the future of transportation.

Specifications:

  • Type: Electric Sedan
  • Horsepower: 670 hp
  • Range: 396 miles
  • Price: $94,990

Performance Highlights:

The Tesla Model S can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in just 2.1 seconds, making it one of the fastest production cars on the market. Its spacious interior and state-of-the-art infotainment system ensure a premium driving experience.
