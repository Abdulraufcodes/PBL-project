Audi R8

The Audi R8 is a high-performance sports car that combines luxury, precision, and power. Its mid-engine layout and quattro all-wheel-drive system make it a joy to drive on any road or track.

Specifications:

  • Type: Sports Coupe
  • Horsepower: 562 hp
  • 0-60 mph: 3.4 seconds
  • Price: $158,600

Performance Highlights:

The Audi R8 offers a perfect balance between refinement and raw performance. Its naturally aspirated V10 engine and responsive handling ensure an exhilarating driving experience.
