Ford Mustang

The Ford Mustang is an iconic American muscle car known for its raw power and timeless design. With a roaring V8 engine and sporty handling, the Mustang continues to captivate driving enthusiasts around the world.

Specifications:

  • Type: Sports Coupe
  • Horsepower: 450 hp
  • 0-60 mph: 4.2 seconds
  • Price: $43,995

Performance Highlights:

The Ford Mustang offers a thrilling driving experience with responsive steering and robust acceleration. It's equally suitable for city cruising or high-speed highway drives.
